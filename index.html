How it works

A 10 means
something specific

You define what a perfect score looks like — then each month, you measure yourself against your own standard.

1

Define your 10

For each life area, write out the specific goals that would make it a 10. You set the standard.

2

Check in monthly

Walk through each area and mark each goal as Yes, In Progress, or No. Flourish calculates your score.

3

See your wheel

Your completed balance wheel shows you exactly where you're thriving — and where you need to focus.

4

Track your growth

Month-over-month trends reveal your trajectory so you can celebrate progress and course-correct.
